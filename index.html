Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline depends on the complexity of your project. A basic website typically takes 1-2 days, while more complex projects can take a week or longer. After our initial consultation, we will provide you with a detailed timeline.

What do I need to provide to get started?

To get started, you'll need to provide:

1) Your business information (logo, brand colors, etc.)

2) Website content (text, images, etc.)

3) Any specific features or functionality you require

4) Examples of websites you like for reference

Will our website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All websites we build are fully responsive, meaning they'll look and function perfectly on all devices including desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

What's your pricing structure?

I offer custom pricing based on your specific needs. Basic websites start at $15, while more complex projects can range from $30 to $50. After discussing your requirements, I'll provide a detailed quote with no obligation.
